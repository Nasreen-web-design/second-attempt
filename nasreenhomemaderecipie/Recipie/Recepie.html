Nasreen's Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

This is a homemade family chocolate chip cookie recipe. My grandmother invented it. It stayed within our family for many years. The one I bake seems to be the yummiest and everybody in my family love them. they call me chef Nasreen.

You can make a lighter version as well by using less butter.

This homemade recipe makes about 12-14 cookies.depending on the size of dough per cookie (1 TBSP dough per cookie).

Home made chocolate chip cookies Recipe:

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup (115 g) unsalted butter, melted
  • ¼ cup(100g) granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup(50g) brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup(125g) all purpose flour
  • ¼ cup (100g) unsweetened coco powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 cup (175g) semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F (175C)
    2. Line baking sheets with parchment paper
  2. In a large bowl, combine melted butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract
  3. Stir in flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt until just combined
  4. Fold in chocolate chips
  5. Drop dough by rounded spoonful onto baking sheet
  6. Bake for 8-10 minutes, until set

Cool and enjoy
